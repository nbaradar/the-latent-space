# Politics
## [Pete Hegseth Says the Pentagon's New Chatbot Will Make America 'More Lethal'](https://www.404media.co/pete-hegseth-says-the-pentagons-new-chatbot-will-make-america-more-lethal/)
> - Secretary of War Pete Hegseth announced the rollout of [GenAI.mil](https://genai.mil) in a [video posted to X](https://x.com/SecWar/status/1998408545591578972?ref=404media.co)
> - GenAI.mil appears to be a custom chatbot interface for [[Alphabet (Google)|Google]] [[Gemini]] that can handle some forms of sensitive—but not classified—data.
> - "There is no prize for second place in the global race for [[Artificial Intelligence (AI)]] dominance," said Emil Michael, Under Secretary of War for Research and Engineering.

# Industry
## [Introducing Mistral 3](https://mistral.ai/news/mistral-3)
> - This release includes three state-of-the-art small, dense models (14B, 8B, and 3B)
> - And [[Mistral]] Large 3 – our most capable model to date – a sparse mixture-of-experts trained with 41B active and 675B total parameters.
> - All models are open-source under Apache 2.0

> - [[Mistral]] has released new dev models/tools
> - **Devstral 2**: 123B-parameter dense transformer supporting a 256K context window.
