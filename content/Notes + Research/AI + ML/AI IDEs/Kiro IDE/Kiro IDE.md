**Kiro** is an AI IDE owned by Amazon that leverages AI agents and [[Spec-Driven Development]] for a simpler dev experience. Kiro is unique due to features like **SPECS** and **HOOKS**

> Amazon told VentureBeat that “Kiro is a general-purpose agentic IDE for developers to work with any platform of their choice,” as opposed to Q Developer, which is more limited in its support for [third-party IDEs](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/amazonq/latest/qdeveloper-ug/q-in-IDE.html#supported-ides-features), restricted to VSCode, JetBrains, Eclipse and Visual Studio.
