# What is AGI?
Sometimes called **human‑level intelligence AI**—is a type of [artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_intelligence "Artificial intelligence") that would match or surpass human capabilities across virtually all cognitive tasks.

Researchers argue that state‑of‑the‑art [large language models](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Large_language_model "Large language model") already exhibit early signs of AGI‑level capability, while others maintain that genuine AGI has not yet been achieved.
