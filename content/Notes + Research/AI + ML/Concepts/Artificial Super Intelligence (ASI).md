# What is ASI?
Artificial Super Intelligence is a concept that is not currently available in the real world and does not exist in any form. This is what people think of when they reference AI overlords or Cyborgs from science fiction movies. Think of HAL 9000 from space odyssey, Skynet from Terminator, VIKI from I, Robot, or Ava from Ex-Machina.
