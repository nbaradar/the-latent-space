An [**autonomous agent**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Autonomous_agent) is an [[Artificial Intelligence (AI)]] system that can perform complex tasks independently. It is advanced in that it **can understand and respond to inquiries, then take action without human intervention**. 

Examples: 
- Self-driving cars
- Customer service chatbots
- Autonomous warehouse robots
