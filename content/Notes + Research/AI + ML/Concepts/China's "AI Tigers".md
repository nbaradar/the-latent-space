The "Six AI Tigers" (大模型六小虎) is a collective designation for six [China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China "China")-based [[Artificial Intelligence (AI)]] startup companies from 2021-2024.

These are considered the top companies in Chinas AI industry, outside of the tech giants Baidu and ByteDance.

https://qz.com/china-six-tigers-ai-startup-zhipu-moonshot-minimax-01ai-1851768509

- Zhipu AI
- Moonshot AI
- [[MiniMax]]
- Baichuan Intelligence
- StepFun
- 01.AI

While Z.ai (Zhipu AI) and MiniMax pursued public listings on the [Hong Kong Stock Exchange](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong_Stock_Exchange "Hong Kong Stock Exchange"), others like Moonshot AI focused on specific consumer applications or remained private with significant cash reserves
