[Microsoft](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft) is an American tech conglomerate headquartered in Redmond, Washington. 

They have influence in the AI industry at a large scale, even if indirectly. In addition to what they own below, they also own GitHub and Copilot (an LLM wrapper). 

![[Pasted image 20250829175957.png]]
