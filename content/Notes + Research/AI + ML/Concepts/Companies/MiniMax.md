**[MiniMax](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MiniMax_(company))** is an [[Artificial Intelligence (AI)]] company based in "Shanghai", China. As of 2024, it has been dubbed one of [[China's "AI Tigers"|China's "AI Tiger"]] companies by investors.

[Miniax M2](https://www.minimax.io/news/minimax-m2), their OpenSource AI model born for Agentic Coding was released in October 2025. You can find the [quantized model here ](https://huggingface.co/models?other=base_model:quantized:MiniMaxAI/MiniMax-M2)
