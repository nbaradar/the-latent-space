[Mistral AI SAS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mistral_AI) is a French [[Artificial Intelligence (AI)]] company based in Paris. Founded in 2023, it has open-weight [[Large Language Model|Large Language Models]] (LLMs), with both open-source and proprietary AI models. As of 2025 the company has a valuation of more than US$14 billion after its series C funding round in September 2025

In December of 2025, the released their latest LLM, [Mistral 3](https://mistral.ai/news/mistral-3). This release includes three state-of-the-art small, dense models (14B, 8B, and 3B) and Mistral Large 3, a sparse mixture-of-experts trained with 41B active and 675B total parameters.
