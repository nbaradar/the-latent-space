American [technology company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Technology_company "Technology company") headquartered in [Santa Clara, California](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Santa_Clara,_California). Founded in 1993 by [[Jensen Huang]] (president and CEO).

develops [graphics processing units](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graphics_processing_unit "Graphics processing unit") (GPUs), [systems on chips](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/System_on_a_chip "System on a chip") (SoCs), and [application programming interfaces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Application_programming_interface "Application programming interface") (APIs) for data science, high-performance computing, and mobile and automotive applications.

Nvidia is considered part of the [Big Tech](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_Tech "Big Tech") group, alongside [[Microsoft]], [Apple](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Inc. "Apple Inc."), [Alphabet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alphabet_Inc. "Alphabet Inc."), [Amazon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amazon_\(company\) "Amazon (company)"), and [Meta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meta_Platforms "Meta Platforms").
