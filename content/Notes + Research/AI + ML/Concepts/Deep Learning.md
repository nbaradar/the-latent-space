In [[Machine Learning]], [**deep learning**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deep_learning) is **a type of machine learning that uses artificial neural networks to learn from data**. It focuses on utilizing multilayered [[Neural Network|Neural Networks]]to perform tasks such as [[Classification]], [[Regression]], and [representation learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Representation_learning "Representation learning").
