**Hangzhou DeepSeek Artificial Intelligence Basic Technology Research Co., Ltd.** [doing business as](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trade_name "Trade name") **DeepSeek**, is a Chinese [artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_intelligence "Artificial intelligence") company that develops [[Large Language Model|Large Language Models]]. 

Its training cost was reported to be significantly lower than other LLMs. The company claims that it trained its V3 model for `US$6 million`—far less than the `US$100 million` cost for [[OpenAI|OpenAI's]] [GPT-4](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GPT-4 "GPT-4") in 2023—and using approximately one-tenth the computing power consumed by [Meta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meta_Platforms "Meta Platforms")'s comparable model, [Llama 3.1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Llama_3.1 "Llama 3.1") DeepSeek's success against larger and more established rivals has been described as "upending AI".
