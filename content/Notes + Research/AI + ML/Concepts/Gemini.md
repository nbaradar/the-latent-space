**Gemini**, formerly known as **Bard**, is a [generative artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generative_artificial_intelligence "Generative artificial intelligence") [chatbot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chatbot "Chatbot") developed by [Google](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google "Google"). Based on the [large language model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Large_language_model "Large language model") (LLM) [of the same name](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gemini_\(language_model\) "Gemini (language model)"), it was launched in 2023 in response to the rise of [OpenAI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenAI "OpenAI")'s [ChatGPT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ChatGPT "ChatGPT"). It was previously[_[when?](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style/Dates_and_numbers#Chronological_items "Wikipedia:Manual of Style/Dates and numbers")_] based on the [LaMDA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LaMDA "LaMDA") and [PaLM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PaLM "PaLM") LLMs.
