**Gemini**, formerly known as **Bard**, is a [generative artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generative_artificial_intelligence "Generative artificial intelligence") [chatbot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chatbot "Chatbot") developed by [[Alphabet (Google)]]. Based on the [[Large Language Model]] [of the same name](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gemini_(language_model)), it was launched in 2023 in response to the rise of [[OpenAI]]'s [ChatGPT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ChatGPT "ChatGPT"). It was previously based on the [LaMDA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LaMDA "LaMDA") and [PaLM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PaLM "PaLM") LLMs.
