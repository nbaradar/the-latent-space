French-American startup company([wiki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hugging_Face)) founded in 2016 based in [New York City](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_tech_companies_in_the_New_York_metropolitan_area "List of tech companies in the New York metropolitan area") that develops tools for building applications using [[Machine Learning]].

Most notable for their [[Transformer]] library, with 1000's of open-source models: https://huggingface.co/models

On April 28, 2021, the company launched the [BigScience Research Workshop](https://bigscience.huggingface.co/) and released the model [[BLOOM]] a year later.

As they look to the future, they want to be the de-facto platform for [open-source robotics](https://www.wired.com/story/hugging-face-acquires-open-source-robot-startup/)
