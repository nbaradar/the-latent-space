[Nano Banana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nano_Banana) is an image generation and editing model powered by [[Generative AI]]. It was created by the [[Alphabet (Google)|Google DeepMind Team]]. It is a text-to-speech variation of their SOTA [[Gemini]] model. 

Nano Banana first appeared publicly as an anonymous model on the crowd-sourced AI evaluation platform [LMArena](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LMArena "LMArena") on August 12th

On November 20th, 2025, DeepMind released Nano Banana Pro (Gemini 3.0 Pro Image) with improved text rendering and world knowledge.
