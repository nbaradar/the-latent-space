Former cofounder and head of [Google Brain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google_Brain), and former Chief Scientist at [Baidu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baidu "Baidu") where he was leading a team of several thousand people. Known for democratizing deep learning through online classes on [Coursera](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coursera "Coursera") and DeepLearning.AI.
