[Blaise Agüera y Arcas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federated_learning)is a vice president, fellow, and the CTO of Technology & Society at [[Alphabet (Google)|Google]], where he leads the Paradigms of Intelligence (Pi) team on basic research in AI and related fields. He is known for his work developing on-device machine learning and for inventing [federated learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federated_learning "Federated learning").
