Known for [his leadership](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Business_career_of_Elon_Musk "Business career of Elon Musk") of [Tesla](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla,_Inc. "Tesla, Inc."), [SpaceX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SpaceX "SpaceX"), [X (formerly Twitter)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/X_\(formerly_Twitter\) "X (formerly Twitter)"), and the [Department of Government Efficiency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Department_of_Government_Efficiency "Department of Government Efficiency") (DOGE). Co-founded [[OpenAI]] to advance [artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_intelligence "Artificial intelligence") research but later left; growing discontent with the organization's direction and their leadership in the [AI boom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_boom "AI boom") in the 2020s led him to establish [xAI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/XAI_\(company\) "XAI (company)").
