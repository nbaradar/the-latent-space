He is the CEO of [[Microsoft]] AI, and the co-founder and former head of applied AI at [DeepMind](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DeepMind "DeepMind"), an AI company acquired by [Google](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google "Google"). After leaving DeepMind, he co-founded [Inflection AI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inflection_AI "Inflection AI"), a [machine learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_learning "Machine learning") and [generative AI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generative_artificial_intelligence "Generative artificial intelligence") company, in 2022.
