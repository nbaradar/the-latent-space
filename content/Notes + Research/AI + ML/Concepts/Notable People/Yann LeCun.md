French-American computer scientist who is well known for his work on Optical Character Recognition and Computer Vision using [[Convolutional Neural Network]] (CNNs). Currently serves as the Chief AI scientist at [[Meta AI]]. In 2018, he along with [[Geoffrey Hinton]] and [[Yoshua Bengio]] won the [ACM A.M. Turing Award](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turing_Award) for their foundational work on [[Deep Learning]]. They are sometimes referred to as the "Godfathers of AI."
