Whisper is an automatic speech recognition (ASR) system from [[OpenAI]]  that was trained on 680,000 hours of multilingual and multitask supervised data collected from the web.

Released as open-source software in September 2022., it is capable of transcribing speech in English and multiple other languages, and can translate several non-English languages into English.

Whisper is a [weakly-supervised](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weak_supervision "Weak supervision") [[Deep Learning]] [acoustic model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acoustic_model "Acoustic model"), made using an [encoder-decoder](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transformer_\(machine_learning_model\)#Encoder/decoder_architecture "Transformer (machine learning model)") [[Transformer|transformer architecture|]]
