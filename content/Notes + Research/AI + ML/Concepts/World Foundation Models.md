World foundation models (WFMs) are neural networks that simulate real-world environments as videos and predict accurate outcomes based on text, image, or video input. [Physical AI](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/glossary/generative-physical-ai/) developers use WFMs to generate custom [synthetic data](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/glossary/synthetic-data-generation/) or downstream AI models for training [robots](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/industries/robotics/) and [autonomous vehicles](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/solutions/autonomous-vehicles/)
